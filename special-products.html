Product Range

AVK Special Products

A range of protective, specialty and sustainable bituminous products beyond standard binders. Standards shown are the relevant specifications; contact us for product datasheets.

Bitumen Primer

Solvent-based bituminous primer conforming to IS 3384. Penetrates and seals porous concrete and metal surfaces. No heating required.

Request a quote →

Anti-Corrosive Bitumen Paint

Black solvent-borne bitumen paint exceeding IS 9862 — waterproof, weatherproof and corrosion-resistant for iron, steel, concrete and timber.

Request a quote →

Polymer-Modified Bond Coat

Polymer-modified cationic bitumen emulsion conforming to EN 13808 for tack coat and fog seal on roads, runways and taxiways — grade selected by pavement temperature.

Request a quote →

Joint Sealant & Spray Injection

Hot-applied polymer-modified joint sealant exceeding IS 1834 for joints in concrete roads, runways and utility cuts, plus RS1 (JP) emulsion for spray-injection repair.

Request a quote →

Coloured Bituminous Surfacing

A translucent synthetic binder for coloured hot-mix asphalt in any shade — ideal for cycle tracks, parking zones, sports courts, tunnels and urban design.

Sustainable & Specialty

Bio-based emulsions for greener road construction and dust-control emulsions for unpaved roads, haul roads and mining sites.
