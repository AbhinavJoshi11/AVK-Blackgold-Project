Chip Seal — Durable, Sustainable Roads

Chip seal is a proven pavement-preservation method — an asphalt emulsion is applied to the existing surface, followed by a layer of aggregate chips rolled to embed them, creating a durable, cost-effective surface.

We bring modern chip-seal equipment to ensure precision, efficiency and quality in every application.

Advantages

  • Cost-effective pavement preservation
  • Suitable for a wide range of conditions
  • Increased skid resistance
  • Prevents water penetration
  • Anti-glare surface
