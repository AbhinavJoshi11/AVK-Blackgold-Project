Performance Benefits

Key Advantages of CRMB over Conventional Bitumen

CRMB delivers measurable, field-proven performance advantages across every stage of pavement life — from construction through long-term service under heavy traffic.

Temperature Stability

Lower susceptibility to daily and seasonal variation — resistant to softening in summer and brittle cracking in winter.

Prevents Rutting

Higher resistance to deformation at elevated pavement temperatures and under slow, heavy traffic.

Higher Fatigue Life

High elastic recovery — the pavement flexes and recovers under repeated loading rather than cracking prematurely.

Better Adhesion

Superior aggregate–binder adhesion for longer pavement life, higher strength and structural stability.

Water & Edge Resistance

Better water resistance reduces stripping and moisture damage; better edge resistance reduces ravelling at joints.

Crack Resistance

Delays cracking and reflective cracking — particularly effective for SAMI applications over old cracked pavements.

High Skid Resistance

Improved skid resistance on wet roads — enhancing safety on expressways, bridges and airport runways.

Eco-Friendly

Manufactured using recycled crumb rubber from end-of-life tyres — supporting India's circular-economy goals.
