Application Guide

How to Repair a Pothole with AVK Products

Both Re-Active solutions can be applied by road-maintenance teams without specialist training.

Re-Active Cold Mix — General Roads

1

Mark & Cut

Mark the pothole and surrounding weak area; cut edges to a square or rectangular shape.

2

Remove Debris

Remove loose material and any stagnant water from the damaged area.

3

Apply Tack Coat

Apply emulsion tack coat to slightly above the adjoining road surface.

4

Fill

Fill the pothole with Re-Active Cold Mix, allowing for compaction.

5

Compact

Sprinkle sand / rock dust over the filled area; compact with hand rammer or roller until flush.

6

Open Traffic

Open to traffic after ~30 minutes. Consume the full bag once opened.

Re-Active Max — Heavy-Duty Roads

1

Remove Debris

Remove loose debris, crumbled asphalt and contamination from the pothole.

2

Dry with Burner

Dry the surface and pothole thoroughly using a propane burner; ensure no moisture.

3

Fill Granulate

Fill the pothole with Re-Active Max granulate; for deep potholes, apply in layers.

4

Melt with Burner

Melt the granulate evenly with the burner, or use pre-melted material from a cooker.

5

Compact Firmly

Compact the repair firmly and uniformly, flush with the surrounding surface.

6

Immediate Use

Road is ready immediately after application and compaction — no waiting.
