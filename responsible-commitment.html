Upholding Responsible Business Practices

Our commitment to responsible business is evident across Corporate Social Responsibility, Sustainability, Health & Safety, Risk Management and Whistleblower protection. AVK Blackgold Infra upholds high standards of ethical conduct and works to positively impact society and the environment.

Responsible Commitments

Creating Positive Change & Community Empowerment

We are committed to creating significant and sustainable value by positively impacting the environment and supporting the communities around us.

Supporting education and vocational skilling programmes that improve livelihoods in the communities near our operations.

Investing in local infrastructure, sanitation and health initiatives that deliver lasting community benefit.

Road-preservation work that extends pavement life, reduces resource use and supports safer travel.

Ethical Practices & Stakeholder Value

We conduct our business to the highest ethical standards and recognise sustainability as core to long-term success.

We comply with applicable laws and implement controls to minimise the environmental impact of our operations, including lower-energy cold-application technologies.

We source from established, responsible partners and expect fair, transparent conduct across our supply chain.

Clear governance and standard operating procedures guide how we work and how decisions are made.

We invest in greener, more durable bituminous technologies that reduce life-cycle cost and emissions.

Prioritising HSE in Responsible Operations

Health, safety and environment protection is one of our fundamental core values, integrated into our processes from design to daily operations.

We provide a safe working environment with hazard identification and risk assessment (HIRA), safety audits, drills and periodic training to prevent incidents.

We work to reduce emissions, waste and energy use across our plants and project sites.

We maintain compliance with regulations and continually improve our HSE performance.

Mitigating Risks for Sustainable Success

Risk management is integral to our operations — we follow a systematic approach to proactively identify, evaluate and address potential risks.

We conduct regular risk assessments across operations, including HIRA and environmental impact assessments.

We put mitigation measures and emergency-preparedness plans in place to protect people, assets and continuity.

Supply and operational risks are managed so customers experience reliable, uninterrupted service.

Transparency & Accountability

We maintain a robust whistleblower channel so employees and stakeholders can report concerns or wrongdoing without fear of retaliation.

We protect the identity of whistleblowers and the information provided, except where disclosure is required by law.

No retaliation is tolerated against anyone who raises a concern in good faith.

Concerns can be reported confidentially to info@blackgoldinfra.com.

All reports are investigated thoroughly, with appropriate action taken in a timely and fair manner.
