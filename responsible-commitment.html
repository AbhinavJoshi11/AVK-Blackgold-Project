Responsible Commitment

Our commitments to community, environment, health and safety, risk and ethics.

Corporate social responsibility

We invest in the communities around our operations through programmes in education, skilling and local development, and through road-preservation work that delivers safer, longer-lasting infrastructure.

Sustainability

We work to reduce the environmental footprint of our products and processes, including cold-application technologies that lower energy use.

Health, safety & environment

Safety is built into how we operate — from plant procedures to site support — with the goal of zero harm to people and the environment.

Risk management

We identify, assess and manage operational and supply risks so that our customers experience reliable, uninterrupted service.

Whistleblower

We maintain a confidential channel for raising concerns about unethical conduct. Reports can be sent to info@blackgoldinfra.com.
